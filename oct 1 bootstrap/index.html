Hyderabad is rich in history and culture, offering a variety of tourist attractions that cater to different interests. Here are some must-visit places in Hyderabad.
Charminar

A symbol of Hyderabad, the Charminar is a 16th-century mosque known for its stunning architecture and historical significance. It’s surrounded by bustling markets, making it a lively place to explore.

Must Visit
Golkonda Fort

This historical fort is known for its impressive architecture and acoustics. Visitors can enjoy a trek up to the fort for panoramic views of the city. The sound and light show in the evenings is a highlight.

Must Visit
Chowmahalla Palace

Once the seat of the Nizams of Hyderabad, Chowmahalla Palace is a magnificent complex showcasing Indo-European architecture. The palace features beautifully landscaped gardens, stunning courtyards, and a rich collection of art and antiques.

Must Visit
Ramoji Film City

One of the largest film cities in the world, Ramoji Film City offers guided tours, live shows, and various attractions related to the film industry. It’s perfect for families and movie enthusiasts.

Must Visit

Birla Mandir

Built with white marble, Birla Mandir is a stunning temple dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara. It offers breathtaking views of the city and is renowned for its intricate carvings and peaceful ambiance.

Must Visit
Jagannath Temple

Located in Banjara Hills, this modern temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath is a replica of the famous temple in Puri. With its striking red-and-white spires and elaborate carvings, it’s a beautiful and serene place of worship.

Must Visit
Peddamma Gudi

Situated in Jubilee Hills, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Peddamma, an incarnation of Shakti. It is particularly popular during the Bonalu festival and attracts thousands of devotees every year.

Must Visit
Chilkur Balaji Temple

Also known as the “Visa Balaji Temple,” Chilkur Balaji is famous for fulfilling devotees’ wishes related to visas and foreign travel. The temple does not accept monetary donations.

Must Visit
Yadadri Temple

Located about 60 km from Hyderabad, Yadadri is a popular pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Lakshmi Narasimha. The newly renovated temple complex is grand and serene.

Must Visit
